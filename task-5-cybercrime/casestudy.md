# Topic: UPI / Online Payment Fraud
One of the cybercrimes prevalent in today’s society is UPI or online payment fraud. In this type of cybercrime, the fraudster tricks the victims into sharing their sensitive information or making false payment requests. For example, in UPI fraud, the victim, who is also a student in this case, is tricked into sharing their sensitive information by receiving a message or call that they have won a prize or that their bank account information is to be verified.
However, the scammer shares the payment request link or code with the victim.
Thus, the fraud is committed in stages when the victim unknowingly authorizes the payment request by clicking the link or scanning the code and sharing their sensitive information, after which money is transferred instantly from their account.
Students and elderly people are the victims of UPI fraud as they are not fully aware of such types of fraud.
Thus, the consequences of UPI fraud include financial loss and stress.
This case emphasizes the significance of awareness in dealing with online transactions.
