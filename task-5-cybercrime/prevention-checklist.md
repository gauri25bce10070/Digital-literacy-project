# Stay Safe Online Checklist
- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords
- Do not scan unknown QR codes
- Always verify payment requests
- Use trusted apps only
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid clicking suspicious links
- Keep apps and devices updated
- Be careful with unknown calls/messages
# UPI Safety (important)
- Never approve “collect requests” without checking
- Do not share UPI PIN with anyone
